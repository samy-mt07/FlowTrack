What's in the app

Five pieces, one loop: plan, focus, break, log, repeat.

boards

Multiple boards

Keep separate boards per project or context, and switch between them without losing your place.

kanban

Kanban view

Drag tasks across columns with priority, due dates, and tags visible at a glance.

timer

Focus & break timer

Run a real timer alongside your board — no separate app, no tab-switching to stay accountable.

history

Session log

Every focus block is recorded automatically, tied to the task you were actually working on.

timeline

Timeline view

See your day laid out chronologically — what got worked on, when, and for how long.

tags

Priority & tags

Sort what matters with due dates, priority flags, and custom tags across every board.

A typical block

Built around how deep work actually happens.

  • 01

    Pick a task from the board

    Open the kanban view, grab the next priority item, drag it into "In progress."

  • 02

    Start a focus session

    The timer runs right next to the board — no context switch to a separate timer app.

  • 03

    Take the break

    Flowlog logs the session and drops you into a break timer automatically.

  • 04

    Review the timeline

    At the end of the day, the timeline view shows exactly where the hours went.
